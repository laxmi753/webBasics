Ambikeshwari College

Ambikeshwari is a unique institution designed to disseminate quality education to students.

Ambikeshwari College is offering a Bachelor of Art Bachelor of Legislative law program of five years at Rajarshi Janak University. This program provides comprehensive theoretical and practical knowledge and skills of legal areas from the dimension of socio-political lens.
